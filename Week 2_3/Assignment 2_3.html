Caring for a pet Lizard!

Habitat

An aquarium or reptile terrarium much larger than the creature will be the best choice.
Add large sand, rocks, branches, and plants to create a natural environment so your reptile friend can feel at home.

Water

Deserts are pretty dry (duh), but water is still important to them.
Give them a shallow dish of fresh water for drinking and soaking. Change the water regularly to keep it clean.

Diet

I hope nobody is feeding them cheese burgers! Feed your lizard a balanced diet of insects, fruits, and vegetables. Some are Insectivorous, Herbivores, and even Omnivorous. Do your research!
Dust food with calcium powder for added nutrition if needed.

Environment

Don't just put them in a room that feels warm to you! Every lizard is different, so it's important to research their specific needs.
Typically, you should maintain a warm and humid environment that changes day and night. Use a heat lamp to create a basking area and mist the enclosure regularly.
